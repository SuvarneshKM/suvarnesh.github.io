Celebrating Hacktoberfest 2020 with the open-source community

A simple way to showcase your bio/ resume to the world. Add your bio to our repo and we'll host in publicly for you. You can share the link to your friends and have fun!

Guidelines for making a PR

1. Fork the repo and open it in your local environment

2. Add a file yourname.html(your resume/ bio) to this repo inside members directory.

3. Create a PR and that's it!
